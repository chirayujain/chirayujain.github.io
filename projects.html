hackBCA

The most successful HS hackathon on the planet

For its 4th year running, hackBCA will be one of the most innovative highschool hackathon again. Created and planned solely by students, hackBCA is open to all high school students who are interested in exploring computer science regardless of skill level. I have been the main hardware director for the past two years, and this year I am a main board member in charge of hardware as well as outreach to sponsors. We have consistently had over 500 high schoolers attend 3:2 male to female ratio. We have worked with major companies such as Intel, Reddit, Vine, Github, and Wolfram Alpha to provide the best hackathon experience for everyone.
